Features of the application

  1. To Do :
    • Add task.
    • Mark task as completed.
    • Remove task.
    • Add reminder for a task.
    • Set a specific date based todo.
    • Use voice command for your convenience.
    • Get feedbacks, based on the ratio of completed & uncompleted tasks.
  2. Journal :
    • Maintain daily journal.
    • Maintain event based journal.
    • Add images to the journal.
    • Explore both reading & writing modes.
  3. Wallet :
    • Add expense and earnign with a specific tag
    • Get the list of your earnigs and expenses
    • A statistics of you earnigs and expenses
    • Use voice command for your convenience
  4. Reminder && Alarm :
    • Add different types of events and set alarm for that event.
    • Disable or enable snooze option for an alarm.
    • Daily and one time alarm functionality.
  5. Online functionalities :
    • Sync will backup your data. When you sign In into the application your previous data will be retrieved.
    • When a crisis hits, your valuable data will be retreieved and your workflow won't be disturbed.
